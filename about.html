Hi, I’m Mansi Garhwal Architect (B.Arch 2020)
Explorer at heart — I believe architecture should be thoughtful, joyful, and meaningful.
I trained as an architect in India, where I gained my degree and registration, now recognised as equivalent to ARB Part II here. Over the past eight years, I have built a diverse portfolio spanning residential, institutional, commercial, and interior projects.
Along the way, I’ve worked beside talented architects and designers who have influenced my approach. Each project has strengthened my belief that good design should be both purposeful and humane.
